Our Story

Founded in 2018 by Adebola Ogunlesi, Elegance was born from a passion for celebrating Nigeria's rich cultural heritage through meticulously crafted events. What began as a small team of dedicated professionals has blossomed into Nigeria's premier luxury event planning company.

Our journey is rooted in understanding the intricate tapestry of Nigerian traditions while delivering experiences that transcend expectations. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we infuse each event with cultural significance, contemporary elegance, and unparalleled attention to detail.

Today, we proudly serve clients across Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, and beyond, including Nigerian diaspora seeking to reconnect with their heritage through meaningful celebrations.
