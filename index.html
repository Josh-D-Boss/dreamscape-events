About Dreamscape Events

From Inspired Beginnings to Unforgettable Experience

Dreamscape Events began as a shared vision between three creatives Divine C. U, Joshua C. Charlington, and John O. Oluwatobi united by a passion for creating extraordinary moments.

What started as a passion project in 2023 became an official brand in 2024, dedicated to elevating Nigerian celebrations through intentional design, flawless execution, and cultural excellence. Whether it's a wedding, corporate gathering, or luxury social event, our mission is simple: to turn your dream into a stunning, unforgettable experience.
